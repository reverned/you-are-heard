Did You Know?

Understanding mental health helps us support ourselves and others better.

1 in 5 adults in the U.S. experience mental illness each year.
60% of people with mental health issues don't seek help due to stigma or lack of access.
Talking to someone, even briefly, can reduce stress by up to 40%.
People facing discrimination or isolation are 3x more likely to experience anxiety or depression.
